Italian Sausage Soup

Description

The Italian Sausage Soup is made in just one pot and takes 10 minutes to prepare. It is an easy dinner that is loaded with flavourful Italian Sausage,tons of veggies, perfectly cooked noodles and parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Italian Sausage
  • 1 clove garlic,minced
  • 2(14 ounce) can stewed tomatoes
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 small zucchini cubed

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Heat a stockpot and Dutch oven over medium high heat
  3. Stir in both tomatoes,carrots and season with salt and pepper
  4. Stir in beans with liquid and zicchini ,cover and simmer until zicchini is tender
  5. Remove soup from heat and add Spinach.Replace the lid and allow spinach to wilt
  6. Stir until warmed through.Serve and enjoy

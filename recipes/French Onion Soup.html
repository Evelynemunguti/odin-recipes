French Onion Soup Gratinee

Description

A french onion soup is delicious.Rich beef stock is kissed with vermouth and brandy then simmered with caramelized onions.Crisp, garlic infused croutonns and melted Gruyere cheese goes on top

Ingredients

  • 4 teaspoons butter
  • 2 large red and sweet onions,thinnly sliced/li>
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1(48 fluid ounce)can chicken broth
  • 1(14 ounce)can beef broth
  • 1 cup red wine

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large pot over medium high heat.Stir in Red Onions,sweet onions and salt
  2. Stir in chicken broth,beef broth,red wine and worcestershire sauce
  3. Set an oven rack about 6 inches from the last saurce and preheat the oven's broiler.
  4. Arrange 4 large oven safe bowls or crocks on a rimmed baking sheet..
  5. Cook under the hot boiler until bubbly and golden brown,about 5 minutes.

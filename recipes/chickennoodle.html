Quick and Easy Chicken Noodle Soup

Description

This Chicken noodle soup recipe can be on the table in just 40 minutes. Whether you are feeling under the weather or you are just in need of a little comfort,this soup will warm you up inside out.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Onion and Celery
  • 4(14.5 ounce)can Chicken broth
  • 1(14.5 ounce)can vegetable broth
  • 1 1/2 cups egg noodles
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried Basil and Oregano

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large pot over medium heat
  2. Add chicken broth,vegetable broth,chicken,egg noodles,carrots,basil,oregano,salt and pepper
  3. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes
